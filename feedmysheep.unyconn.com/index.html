About us

Vision

  1. We envision all children live with equal opportunities and dignity irrespective of their caste, creed and culture.

Mission

  1. To connect people around the world to help children to thrive and excel in lives, and empower them to change their own lives and communities for a peaceful world.

Values

  1. We value the children as the best gifts from God and dedicated to ensuring a peaceful world for them. We are ready to response to any adversities arise.

Objectives

  1. Feed My Sheep Trust Bangladesh, The principal objective of the Trust shall be to promote compassion to children of all communities in Bangladesh also to promote spiritual and mental development, purity, integrity, holiness, Godliness and security. Besides, the Trust shall provide peaceful life to the poor people through Gods love. Feed My Sheep Trust Bangladesh is a non-profitable Trust. Its purpose is to alleviate human suffering and distress and promote human development in all its forms.
  2. To support the poor children in their education through sponsorship programs in each of our working areas & help them to build a skillful life.
  3. To build children home and orphanage for the children, vocational training center for the youths and old homes for the aged people.
  4. To make hospital for the poor people so that they get medical care and facilitates at lower cost, the hospital shall be named JD Hospital Private Ltd.
  5. We encourage the youths and help them to set up clubs.
  6. We work for adolescents to make them aware on health issues and support them through nutrition.
  7. We engage womenfolk to make things of handicrafts through needle works.
  8. We work for women empowerment and undertake activities which promote gender equality and disability inclusion in all aspects.
  9. To work for the betterment of the sex worker and their children based in Dacope, Khulna.
  10. To work for ensuring safe drinking water to the peoples of southern Bangladesh.
  11. To work for food security and alternative livelihoods for costal area people as they are the victim of climate change. We can take initiatives of DRR & CCA .
  12. To work for the marginalized farmers so that they get irrigation facilities and other supports in times of dry season.
  13. We respond to any natural and manmade disasters occur.
  14. We work for establishing peace.
